About our company

Healthcare with Commitment, Compassion, and Care.

HerUniqueHealth Consult is a platform for women’s health and education.

HerUniqueHealth Consult partners with Fertility Education and Medical Management (FEMM), to provide a comprehensive health program to women of all ages who are interested in taking charge of their overall health and wellbeing. We also collaborate with health professionals especially in Nigeria to create awareness about women’s health issues through community engagement. We give regular health classes to women and have carried out free medical outreaches in rural and suburban communities in Enugu state, Edo state and Federal capital territory, Abuja. HerUniqueHealth Consult was formally registered in 2021 with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission in 2021
